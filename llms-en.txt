# Nijenhuis Boat Rental

> Family-run boat rental, sloop hire and boat hire in Wanneperveen, Overijssel. Whisper boats, luxury sloops and boat rentals near Giethoorn. Located directly on Weerribben-Wieden National Park, 10 km from Giethoorn. Open 1 April – 31 October, daily 09:00–18:00. No boating license required. Phone: +31 522 281 528.

Nijenhuis Botenverhuur rents electric sloops (whisper boats), sailboats, canoes, kayaks and SUP boards in the Weerribben near Giethoorn. In addition, seasonal camping with private mooring spots. A family business for over 50 years. Address: Veneweg 199, 7946 LP Wanneperveen, Netherlands.

## Services

- [Boat Rental](https://nijenhuis-botenverhuur.com/botenverhuur.md): Whisper boat, luxury sloop, boat hire. Electric sloops (€125–230/day), sailboats (€70–85/day), canoes (€45/day), kayaks (€35/day), SUP boards. No boating license required. Open April–October.
- [Holiday Home](https://nijenhuis-botenverhuur.com/vakantiehuis.md): Holiday house Belterwiede, 5 bedrooms, max. 12 persons, directly on Lake Belterwijde. Available year-round.
- [Camping](https://nijenhuis-botenverhuur.com/camping.md): Small-scale seasonal camping, 1 April–31 October. Each pitch with private mooring, water, electricity and sewage. Caravan may stay year-round.
- [For Sale](https://nijenhuis-botenverhuur.com/te-koop.md): Chalets and mobile homes for sale on permanent pitches in the Weerribben.
